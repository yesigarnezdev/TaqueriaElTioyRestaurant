Our Story

A True Taste
of Mexico

Taqueria El Tio & Restaurant, located in Aurora, IL, has been serving the surrounding areas of Yorkville, Oswego, Batavia, Geneva, Plano, and beyond since 2012. We specialize in authentic Mexican dishes, including our signature tacos, Bistec a la Mexicana, and mouthwatering Mexican breakfasts.

Whether you're craving a traditional taco or looking to enjoy a flavorful meal with family and friends, our menu offers something for everyone. Visit Taqueria El Tio & Restaurant in Aurora for a true taste of Mexican cuisine—where every bite feels like home!
